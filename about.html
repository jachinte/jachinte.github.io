About

Welcome to the website of Miguel Jim√©nez. I am a PhD student at the University of Victoria, located in the beautiful city of Victoria, Canada.

I received a Bachelor degree in Systems Engineering from Universidad Icesi in 2014, and a Master of Science degree in Informatics and Telecommunications in 2016 from the same university. My undergraduate thesis project (in Spanish) was a domain-specific language (DSL) for specifying and executing composable and automated tests for the Service Component Architecture. For my master thesis, I developed a framework for generating and deploying dynamic performance monitors for Self-Adaptive Systems. The framework was composed of two DSLs, PaSCAni, for dynamic monitoring, and Amelia, for application deployment.

My research interests include Self-Adaptive Software Systems, Domain-Specific Languages, Software Deployment, and their practical application in software development. My professional objective is to find challenging problems, understand them and propose solutions that positively impact engineers' work.
