About

whoami Modified on March 01, 2016

Welcome to the website of Miguel Jiménez. I am a PhD student at the University of Victoria, located in the beautiful city of Victoria, Canada.

I am originally from Colombia, host to the lungs of the world and the mythical land once imagined by Nobel prize-winning author Gabriel García Márquez.

I received a Bachelor degree in Systems Engineering from Universidad Icesi in 2014, and a Master of Science degree in Informatics and Telecommunications in 2016 from the same university.

My research interests include Self-Adaptive Software Systems, Domain-Specific Languages, Software Deployment, and their practical application in software development. My professional objective is to find challenging problems, understand them and propose solutions that positively impact engineers' work.
